Comedian Pep
An Artwork for the New Digital Renaissance

Comedian Pep is a visionary project that reinterprets Cattelan’s concept of provocation through Pepe, the iconic meme, now in a new yellow form. Born as a declaration of rebellion against convention, $PEP aims to bring the first ever Nft minted on Solana to Sotheby’s Metaverse, continuing the path that the original Comedian paved towards new horizons of artistic expression & creative rebellion.

Comedian | Maurizio Cattelan

Comedian Pep | Unknown Artist

In the world of contemporary art, few works have shaken the collective imagination like Maurizio Cattelan’s Comedian, the famous “Banana with Tape”. This simple representation of a fruit taped to a wall challenged traditional definitions of value and artistic meaning, transforming into an icon of provocation & rebellion. It was not just an artwork but a subversive act that made everyone question what art really is and how it can break mental barriers. Now, that symbol of provocation will be auctioned on September 20th by Sotheby’s, once again renewing the message of rebellion that Cattelan engraved into the heart of contemporary culture.

It’s in this spirit that Comedian Pep was born. Inspired by the irony and cultural impact of Comedian, $PEP is much more than a tribute: it’s a provocation and a challenge to artistic and cultural norms. Here, Pepe – the meme symbol of crypto-culture – is reimagined in yellow, transforming into a bold interpretation that honors the original while expanding its meaning. Just as Cattelan broke artistic conventions, $PEP aims to dismantle mental barriers within the world of crypto and NFTs, pushing digital art to the next level.

ART Rebellion

After the launch of $PEP, the next clear and ambitious goal is to mint a unique NFT, Comedian Pep, on the Solana blockchain. This NFT will be auctioned on September 20th, coinciding with the sale of Cattelan’s original work. Anyone will be able to participate in this auction, creating an event that mirrors the audacious rebellion of the past. Just as Comedian showed that anyone could redefine the value of art, Comedian Pep aims to show that anyone can contribute to creating value and wealth. Proceeds from the auction will be partially used to fund $PEP’s listing on Cex and with the remaining funds allocated toward a buyback and burn of the $PEP token, supporting its growth and strengthening the community.

But this is just the first step in Comedian Pep’s journey. After the auction, we will launch a petition open to all who believe in the project and want to unite in a collective mission: to bring the first NFT on Solana to the world’s most iconic auction house, Sotheby’s Metaverse. Just as the Comedian piece broke mental barriers in the art world, Comedian Pep seeks to push the crypto world to new frontiers, with the aspiration of seeing it auctioned by Sotheby’s.

This is not just another crypto project; it’s a social experiment, a demonstration of the power of community and the strength of a bold idea. Comedian Pep is more than a token or an NFT: it is a symbol of how a concept, a community, and a rebellious idea can unite people in a shared mission, pushing the boundaries of culture & digital finance.
